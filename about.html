About Us

ABOUT US

Where Dreams Find a Place to Stay

Welcome to Vila Koal, your tranquil escape in Ksamil just 600 meters from Ksamil Beach. Our cozy rooms and suites offer modern comforts like air conditioning and flat-screen TVs. Enjoy a buffet breakfast, relax in our garden, and explore nearby beaches and Butrint National Park. With 24-hour service and complimentary WiFi, Vila Koal ensures a peaceful stay in the heart of Ksamil.
